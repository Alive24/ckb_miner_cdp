# Presale

## Mining Offering

- Miner propose a mining offering on the basis of staked deposit;
  - Miner gets to set it's own price and volume, against CKB or stable coin;
  - By default proposal comes with a fee to debt;
  - Repay gets boosted if repaying with CKB mined with our pool;
- Buyers mint mUDT (in early stage, only CKB) that can be redeemed for 1:1 UDT at the end of the presale from the protocol;
  - Should set a waiting period; set fee for quick redemption;

## Mining Recruiting (Tokenized Mining)

- Buyers list to buy mUDT at it's own price and volume, against CKB or stable coin;
- Miners can fulfill by minting mUDT on their staked deposit;
- Other users can quickly sell their unredeemed mCKB (with fee) from buyers too;

## Notes

- Both offering and recruiting can be set to perpetual by keeping a balance;
